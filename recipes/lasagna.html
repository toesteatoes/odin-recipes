Lasagna

This is the best LASAGNA RECIPE! Pasta, an easy meat sauce, and ricotta cheese are layered and then topped with more cheese before baking.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz tomato sauce
  • Half cup diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pound mozarella shredded
  • 1 pound sausage

Steps:

  1. Cook sausage and aromatics
  2. Add tomato sauce and seasoning
  3. Place noodles in pan of hot water to soften
  4. Mix cheese and egg
  5. Layer noodles and sauce and cheese in pan
  6. Preheat to 375F and bake for 25 minutes while covered in foil and an addition 30 with foil removed
